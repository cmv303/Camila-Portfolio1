About

CAMILA MOREIRAS (1986) is an hispanic artist who makes experimental and creative documentaries. Her work has been exhibited and screened in festivals and venues such as IDFA, Festival Internacional de Cine en Guadalajara, Laboratorio Arte Alameda, San Diego Underground Festival, and Los Angeles Underground Film Forum (best experimental short 2018). Her film Sine die was shortlisted for Best Short Documentary film for the 2023 Goya Awards.

She is currently taking a Coding Bootcamp and hopes that, among other things, she can one day make this portfolio page more intuitive, pretty, and user friendly!

Moreiras holds a doctorate in Spanish and Portuguese Languages and Literature from New York University, and a master's degree in creative documentary from the Pompeu Fabra University in Barcelona, Spain.
